Quick Ink

❮ ❯

These works are "quick" ink drawings that serve a purpose of selfcare. I can find myself feeling uninspired and getting in ruts but I forced myself to get back into drawing and making. At first it seemed like a task but as I got into the rhythm it became relaxing. I enjoyed knowing that the next day I would have that hour to myself to create whatever. It didn't have to be a super special or meaningful drawing, it was just that time to decompress and get lost in the process.
