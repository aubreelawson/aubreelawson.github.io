paper Cutting

Paper Cutting has become a go to medium when I don't know what to draw. The repretitiveness of the carving almost becomes mesmerizing and calming. That is not to say I have not gotten frustrated at trying to carve some of the weird small parts. I love playing with the negative space and holding it up when I'm done and seeing what background comes through. Also looking at the shadows they create is interesting along with stacking previous carvings.
